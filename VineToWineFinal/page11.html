The Fund

Vine to Wine - The Vineyard Fund is not a Wine Fund.

It is a first of a kind Real Estate and Private Equity Fund, globally investing in Vineyards (Real Estate) and Vineyard related assets (Private Equity) such as Vineyard Management Companies, Wine Storage Facilities and Global Wine Distributors securing steady returns to investors from the production and global distribution of highly marketable wines.
