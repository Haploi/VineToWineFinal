25 June 2020

Due to the lockdown of the COVID19 virus the Fund was forced to postpone its already planned presentation meetings for March, April, May and June in different European countries. Following this the Fund directors unanimously decided to extend the Initial Offering Period till 30 November 2020. New dates for upcoming presentation meetings will be scheduled in consultation with BIL Manage Invest, the Fund´s AIFM and Global Distributor. New dates will be announced asap.
